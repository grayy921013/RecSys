About Abra

We are trying to collect your opinion about whether two movies are similar in order to improve the performance of existing movie recommendation system. Your label submissions will help to make our life easier in selecting movies tonight!

Thanks for your help :)

Privacy Terms

The purpose of this experiment is to collect user opinions to determine the effectiveness and efficiency of various similarity algorithms over movies. This experiment will not "test" you or your ability to select similar movies. Instead, our aim is to evaluate the algorithms with respect to human judgment. Your identity information will not be recorded by participating in this experiment. Please be advised that there is no risk associated with participation in this study.
If you read and fully understood the extent of the study and any risks involved, tick the checkbox "I agree". Your action acknowledges your understanding of the information provided in this form and indicates your willingness to participate in this experiment.
